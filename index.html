How do Video Game Franchises Fare Over Time?

There have been an assortment of significant video game franchises created over the years. These range from household names, such as Mario, Pokemon, or Call of Duty, to lesser-known series, such as Monster Hunter or Forza. However, as franchises age and release more entries, do they decay? Do franchises see lower sales and/or lower critical ratings as they age?

Rating trend: Whether franchise ratings on Metacritic are trending up or down over time. The greater the magnitude of this value the more extreme the trend
